Project 1

Giving voice to the silent, framing the story, and inspiring change agents with influential graphics is our forte’. If you are looking for the team who gets ‘all the right angles’ and brings reality to your visions, look no further! 90 DEGREE FIMZ is a family-oriented company created by Korey Townsend (uncle) of VoicedTV and Jae Harrison (nephew) of JR Productions. The pair boasts more than 10 years of public service to Mecklenburg County through law enforcement, citywide recreation, community outreach, and giving voice to our city’s most vulnerable populace. Relationships fostered with non-profit organizations, schools, restaurants, local entrepreneurs, and even members of disenfranchised communities will serve as the catalyst for the marketing campaign. Coupling resources with video production expertise garnered through completed projects for larger entities including The University of Maryland, The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, The Brookings Institution as well as developing a keen eye for the intricate details found in producing the cultural flavor of local artists to disseminate to the global diaspora not only makes us profound, but it makes us relevant.
